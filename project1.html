Brand Identity Design: The Room AU

2025 Branding, Design, Marketing

Project Overview

The Room AU is a young Australian lighting brand specializing in small to medium decorative lights and home furnishing products. After understanding the brand requirements, I developed hand-drawn logo designs and created comprehensive brand identity and visual guidelines.

Challenge

Creating a distinctive brand identity for a new Australian lighting company that would stand out in the competitive home furnishing market while appealing to modern consumers.

Solution

Developed a comprehensive brand identity system including hand-drawn logo designs, color palette, typography guidelines, and visual language that reflects the brand's commitment to quality lighting and contemporary design.

Results

Successfully established a cohesive brand identity that effectively communicates The Room AU's values and aesthetic, providing a strong foundation for all marketing and branding efforts.
